TO IMPLEMENT QUEUE USING STACK
Considering a queue q1 and two stacks s1 and s2.
First to enqueue the value is pushed to s1.
And to dequeue:
->values from s1 in pushed to s2(considering both the stacks are not empty).
->we have to pop the element from s2 and return it.
By this way the new element is first displayed that is the concept of queue.
APPLICATION OF QUEUES AND STACKS:
QUEUES:
->To handle interupts(like in call centers)
->Used for disk and CPU scheduling
->used to synchronise asynchronised data

STACKS:
->Infix to Postfix or Infix to Prefix Conversion
